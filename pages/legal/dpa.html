Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Effective Date: 05 February 2026

1. Parties

Data Controller: You (the Customer)

Data Processor: Axiosky ("we", "us", "our")

2. Purpose

This DPA governs how Axiosky processes Personal Data on your behalf when you use our Services (Governor, Policy Engine, Orchestrator, and related features).

3. Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • Processing: Any operation performed on Personal Data (collection, storage, use, disclosure, deletion, etc.).
  • Customer Data: Data you upload, process, or transmit through the Services (which may include Personal Data).

4. Scope

Axiosky processes Personal Data only on your documented instructions and solely to provide the Services.

5. Our Obligations as Processor

We will:

  • Process Personal Data only as instructed by you
  • Ensure our staff are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
  • Assist you in responding to Data Subject requests (access, correction, deletion, etc.)
  • Notify you without undue delay in case of a Personal Data breach
  • Help you conduct Data Protection Impact Assessments if required
  • Delete or return all Personal Data upon termination of the Services (within 30 days), except where retention is required by law

6. Sub-processors

We may engage third-party sub-processors (e.g., cloud hosting providers) to help provide the Services.

We will:

  • Maintain a current list of sub-processors
  • Give you at least 30 days' notice before adding a new sub-processor
  • Remain fully liable for any sub-processor's actions

You may object to a new sub-processor. If you object, you may terminate the affected Services.

7. Data Subject Rights

You remain responsible for responding to Data Subject requests.
We will assist you by providing access to the relevant Personal Data within a reasonable time.

8. Data Deletion & Return

Upon termination of the Services:

  • We will delete all Personal Data within 30 days
  • We will provide a written confirmation of deletion upon request
  • Immutable audit logs may be retained only as required by law

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews.

10. Liability

Our liability under this DPA is limited to the amounts specified in our Terms of Service. We are not liable for any loss caused by your instructions or misuse of the Services.

11. Governing Law

This DPA is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Kerala, India.

12. Contact

For questions about this DPA or data processing:

Email: privacy@axiosky.com
