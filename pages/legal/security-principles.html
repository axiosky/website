Security Principles

Last Updated: 05 February 2026

1. Our Security Commitment

At Axiosky, security is foundational. We build and operate our governance platform (Governor, Policy Engine, Orchestrator) with the highest standards of information security, privacy, and reliability.

We are committed to protecting your data and earning your trust every day.

2. Key Security Principles

We follow these core principles:

  • Security by Design — Security is built into every feature from the start, not added later.
  • Least Privilege — Access is granted only to what is strictly needed.
  • Defense in Depth — Multiple layers of protection across people, processes, and technology.
  • Transparency — We are open about our practices and responsive to your questions.
  • Continuous Improvement — We regularly review and strengthen our controls.

3. Data Protection

  • Encryption — All data in transit uses TLS 1.3+. Data at rest is encrypted with AES-256.
  • Access Controls — Role-based access, multi-factor authentication, and regular reviews.
  • Isolation — Your data is logically separated from others.
  • No AI Training on Your Data — We do not use your data to train models.

4. Secure Operations

  • Regular Audits & Testing — Annual penetration testing and vulnerability scans by independent experts.
  • Incident Response — 24/7 monitoring with a documented response plan.
  • Secure Development — Code reviews, dependency scanning, and secure coding practices.
  • Vendor Management — We only work with trusted partners who meet our security standards.

5. Compliance

We design our platform to support compliance with major frameworks including:

  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)
  • Information Technology Rules, 2011 (India)
  • GDPR-ready controls
  • SOC 2 Type II (in process)

We offer deployment options (cloud, on-premises, air-gapped) to meet your regulatory and data residency needs.

6. Your Role

Security is a shared responsibility. We encourage you to:

  • Use strong passwords and enable MFA
  • Keep your access credentials secure
  • Report any suspected security issues immediately to security@axiosky.com

7. Contact Us

For security questions or to report a vulnerability:

Email: security@axiosky.com
