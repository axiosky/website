Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10 March 2026
Last Updated: 10 March 2026
Governing Law: Republic of India

1. Introduction & Data Fiduciary

Axiosky ("we", "us", "our") is a technology company registered in India, providing governance and control infrastructure for autonomous AI systems. For the purposes of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDP Act"), Axiosky acts as the Data Fiduciary in respect of personal data collected through this website.

Registered Address: Kanayannur, Kerala, India. (Full registered address will be updated upon incorporation.)

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you visit axiosky.com, submit inquiries, or engage with us for pilots, consultations, or services. This policy is governed by Indian law, including the DPDP Act 2023 and the Information Technology Act, 2000 and its rules.

Where we collect your personal data through a form or other affirmative action, we will present you with a clear notice of what data is collected and for what purpose at the point of collection. Your submission of that form constitutes your consent to the processing described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you submit the Contact / Demo Request Form:

  • First Name & Last Name
  • Job Title
  • Work Email Address
  • Company / Organisation Name
  • Country
  • Sector / Industry
  • Organisation Size (optional)
  • What You Need Help With (multiple selections)
  • Project or Challenge Description
  • Timeline for Pilot / Deployment
  • How You Heard About Us (optional)

We may also collect:

  • Communications you send us (emails, follow-up messages)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information) automatically collected when you visit the website

3. Legal Basis & How We Use Your Information

We process your personal data on the following bases under the DPDP Act 2023:

  • Consent — you provide consent by submitting a form on our website. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting privacy@axiosky.com.
  • Legitimate use — where processing is necessary to fulfil a service you have requested.

We use the information you provide only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry and schedule consultations or demos
  • To understand your requirements and evaluate fit for a pilot
  • To send you relevant updates about our services, where you have consented to receive them (you may withdraw this consent at any time by emailing privacy@axiosky.com)
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for training AI models. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Data Sharing

We may share your information only in these limited cases:

  • With trusted service providers (email delivery, cloud hosting) who are contractually bound to process data only on our instructions and to maintain confidentiality
  • If required by law, court order, or a lawful government or regulatory directive
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring, with notice to you where practicable and lawfully permissible

We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. International Data Transfers

Some of the service providers we use to operate our website and communications (such as cloud infrastructure or email delivery services) may process or store data on servers located outside India. Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to ensure that such providers maintain appropriate security standards and process data only for the purposes described in this policy. We will update this section with specific details as our infrastructure matures.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is primarily stored in India. We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures — including encryption in transit and access controls — to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your enquiry data for a maximum of 3 years from the date of collection, unless you become a customer (in which case retention is governed by your service agreement) or unless you request earlier deletion.

7. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act 2023

As a Data Principal under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access — obtain a summary of the personal data we hold about you and how it is being processed (Section 11, DPDP Act)
  • Right to Correction & Erasure — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data, or erase personal data that is no longer necessary for the purpose it was collected (Section 12, DPDP Act)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — withdraw consent at any time; withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal
  • Right to Grievance Redressal — raise a grievance with our Grievance Officer (see Section 10); if your grievance is not resolved satisfactorily, you may escalate to the Data Protection Board of India once operational (Section 13, DPDP Act)
  • Right to Nominate — nominate another individual to exercise your rights under this policy in the event of your death or incapacity (Section 14, DPDP Act)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@axiosky.com
